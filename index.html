About Green Haven

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Green Haven Nursery started as a small backyard passion project and has grown into one of the region's most respected plant nurseries.

Our mission is to connect people with nature by providing healthy, sustainable plants and the knowledge needed to care for them. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the joy and benefits of having plants in their lives.

At Green Haven, we practice sustainable growing methods, using organic fertilizers and natural pest control to ensure our plants are healthy for both you and the environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Nursery Management System?

A Nursery Management System is a software solution designed to help plant nurseries organize and optimize their operations, including inventory management, plant care scheduling, customer relationship management, sales tracking, and reporting.

How does the plant tracking feature work?

Our plant tracking feature allows you to maintain detailed records for each plant in your inventory, including species information, growth stage, watering and fertilization schedules, location within the nursery, and health status. You can also track plants from seed to sale with our batch management system.

Can I manage multiple nursery locations with this system?

Yes! Our system supports multi-location management, allowing you to track inventory, sales, and staff across different physical locations. Each location can have its own dashboard and reporting, while still providing consolidated data for management.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, we offer mobile applications for both iOS and Android devices. The mobile apps allow your staff to update plant information, process sales, and receive care reminders while moving throughout the nursery, without needing to return to a desktop computer.

How secure is my customer and business data?

We take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security protocols, regular security audits, and maintain compliance with data protection regulations. Your business and customer information is backed up daily.

Do you offer integration with accounting software?

Yes, our system integrates with popular accounting software solutions including QuickBooks, Xero, and Sage. This allows for seamless transfer of sales data, inventory valuations, and purchase orders between systems.

What kind of customer support do you provide?

We provide 24/7 email support, phone support during business hours, and an extensive knowledge base with video tutorials. Our Premium and Enterprise plans also include dedicated account managers and priority support.

Can I try the system before purchasing?

Absolutely! We offer a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start your trial. We also provide a demo session with our team to help you get started and answer any questions you might have.
