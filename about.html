About Green Haven

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Green Haven Nursery started as a small backyard passion project and has grown into one of the region's most respected plant nurseries.

Our mission is to connect people with nature by providing healthy, sustainable plants and the knowledge needed to care for them. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the joy and benefits of having plants in their lives.

At Green Haven, we practice sustainable growing methods, using organic fertilizers and natural pest control to ensure our plants are healthy for both you and the environment.
